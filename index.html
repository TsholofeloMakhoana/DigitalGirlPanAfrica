WomenTechster Fellowship

A year-long training program that equips young girls and women across Africa with digital and deep tech skills.During the course of theFellowship, fellows undergo intense technical training, mentoring and internship.

All our fellows are encouraged and task with the responsibility of supporting local institutions by providing freeSTEMsensitization sessions for girls within the ages of 9–18years old.
